What is D3adliner?

Deadliner is an application being developed for YHack 2017 at Yale University.

With us, there will be no more "D3adlines"

What it Does

Deadliner is an intelligent scheduling app aimed at College and University Students.

Deadliner allows you to input your goals, deadlines, todos, and it generates a

comprehensive schedule and suggests tips for you based on your daily routines.

Features

-Comprehensive scheduling ~A comprehensive schedule made just for YOU
-Timely reminders ~Helpful reminders to keep you on track in completing your goals
-Intelligent planning ~Just tell us your assignments and D3adliner will help you plan out
the schedule to ace it.
